## BROKEN LINK RECOVERY (Low-Effort, High-ROI)

**Process:**
1. Use Ahrefs "Site Explorer" to find broken links pointing to solar installer sites
2. Use Wayback Machine to recover original content
3. Create superior alternative on VoltSol site
4. Reach out to linking site: "Your [topic] link is broken. We have better version."
5. Secure replacement link

**Example:**
```
Broken link found: SolarBuyersGuide.com linking to outdated "EG4 vs. Victron" comparison (404)
Solution: VoltSol has comprehensive 2026 comparison
Outreach: "Hey, I noticed your EG4 comparison link is broken. We have an updated 2026 
version with current specs + pricing. Would you consider linking instead?"
Result: Backlink acquired (or upgrade from existing link)
Effort: 15 minutes per link
```

**Target 10-15 broken links per month** (January-May, total: 50-75 recoveries)

## CONCLUSION

This link-building strategy prioritizes **quality over quantity**—focusing on high-authority domains (DA 40+) that drive both direct referral value and search ranking improvements.

**Key Success Factors:**
1. **Personalization:** Every pitch is unique, not templated
2. **Value-first:** Offer value to the linking site, not just promotion for VoltSol
3. **Timing:** Align outreach with trending news (tax deadline, outages, solar incentives)
4. **Persistence:** Follow up 2-3x if no response; don't give up after first rejection
5. **Measurement:** Track every link, monitor DA trend monthly

**Expected ROI:**
- 30-45 quality backlinks by Month 5
- Authority score increase: 15 → 40+ (Semrush ranking)
- Organic ranking improvements: 50+ target keywords in top 10
- Lead volume from organic: 30-50/month by year-end
