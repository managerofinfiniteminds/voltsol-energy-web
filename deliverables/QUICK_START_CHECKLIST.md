## IF THINGS GO WRONG (Contingency)

**Scenario 1: Rankings Not Improving (Month 2)**
- Root cause: Check for indexation issues (GSC)
- Fix: Increase content quality (longer, more comprehensive pages)
- Acceleration: Paid ads (Google Ads) to supplement organic

**Scenario 2: Low Lead Volume Despite High Rankings**
- Root cause: Form friction, weak CTAs, poor conversion flow
- Fix: Simplify forms, clearer CTAs, A/B testing
- Quick win: Add live chat for real-time conversions

**Scenario 3: Competitors Respond Aggressively**
- Root cause: Market competition intensifying
- Fix: Double down on differentiation (Hugo local brand, pricing edge, EG4 focus)
- Acceleration: PR push, more YouTube content, press coverage

**Scenario 4: Link Building Stalls**
- Root cause: Pitches not compelling, timing off, or oversaturation
- Fix: Diversify tactics (move to tier-3, broken link recovery, YouTube collabs)
- Acceleration: Hire freelancer for outreach (20+ emails/week)
