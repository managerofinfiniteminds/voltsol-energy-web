## THE SYSTEM — 3 parts, told as one story

### 1. SOLAR PANELS → make the power (free, from the sun)
Generic high-efficiency PV modules. Generate DC from sunlight. Feed the inverter.
- Narrative role: *"The sun shows up for free every day."*

### 2. EG4 HYBRID INVERTER → the brain
The traffic cop. Converts solar DC → home AC, charges the battery, runs off-grid / grid-assist / grid-tied.

**FlexBOSS18** (the value hero — EG4 calls it the cost-effective entry to hybrid solar):
- 13kW continuous AC output (54A @ 240V); 10kW battery-only output
- Up to 18kW usable PV input (24kW max), 2 MPPTs
- 120/240V split-phase, pure sine wave
- EMP-hardened; UL 3141 / UL 1741
- 90A grid bypass; pairs with GridBOSS smart panel
- 29.5 x 20.5 x 10.4 in, 114.6 lbs, **10-yr warranty**
- App: EG4 Connect (phone monitoring)

**Sibling units (for "scales with you" messaging):**
- 12kPV: 12kW PV in / 8kW AC out — smaller homes
- 18kPV-12LV: established workhorse, off-grid/grid-assist/grid-tied + sell-back
- FlexBOSS21: bigger sibling
- 12000XP / 6000XP: pure off-grid line

### 3. EG4 BATTERY → store it (power day AND night)
**WallMount All-Weather (280Ah):**
- 14.3kWh per unit, 48V (51.2V) LiFePO4
- 200A continuous discharge / 10kW power delivery
- **IP65 outdoor-rated** (mounts inside OR outside — NorCal friendly)
- **8,000 cycle life** (decades of daily cycling)
- On-board LCD touchscreen, closed-loop comms with EG4 inverters
- UL 1973 / UL 9540A
- Stackable: 14.3 → 28.6 → 43kWh+ (scales to whole-home + multi-day)
- WallMount 314Ah Indoor: up to 16kWh, 10,000 cycles

### 4. EG4 MINI-SPLIT HEAT PUMP → live on it (heat + cool, efficiently)
**This is the differentiator most solar companies don't sell.** EG4 makes its own.
- Hybrid Solar Mini-Split AC/DC (R32): 12k & 24k BTU — runs partly on DC solar directly
- 24K & 36K Multizone Mini-Split AC/DC
- Standard 9k/12k BTU mini-split heat pumps (R32)
- Heat pumps pair beautifully with solar+battery: variable inverter compressors sip power, no maintenance vs thermal systems
- Narrative role: *"The biggest power hog in your house — heating & cooling — now runs on sunlight you stored."*

## THE NARRATIVE SPINE (for site copy)

**Old way:** Buy power from PG&E forever. Rates only go up. Blackouts aren't your choice.
**Half-measure ("normal solar"):** A $40k loan, still grid-tied, still get a bill, dark in a blackout.
**The VoltSol / EG4 way:** Make it (panels) → Store it (battery) → Live on it (inverter + mini-split). Off-grid capable. Power through blackouts. The biggest energy cost — climate — runs on stored sun.

**Emotional arc:** Anger (the bill) → Curiosity (there's another way?) → Hope (oh, THIS is how it works) → Belief (the number) → Trust (Hugo, real installs) → Action (get my quote).
